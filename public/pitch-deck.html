The Problem

Fundraising through bingo, raffles, and quizzes is popular — but heavily regulated. Charities and clubs risk non-compliance, lost revenue, and reputational damage due to outdated tools and manual processes.

  • 🏢11,500+ charities in Ireland and 168,000+ in the UK
  • ⚖️Legal thresholds for prizes, permits, and age restrictions
  • ⚠️Clubs often unknowingly breach caps or fail to track events properly
  • 🔍No tools currently combine compliance, digital payments, and transparency
2 / 12

Our Solution: FundRaisely

FundRaisely is a digital platform that enables clubs and charities to:

  • 💸Run fully compliant bingo, raffle, lotto, and quiz events
  • 📜Enforce legal restrictions before launch
  • 📊Track and report all fundraising activity
  • 🔐Log events immutably using blockchain (Phase 2)
  • ✅Accept payments via Stripe, PayPal, and Revolut — no crypto needed
